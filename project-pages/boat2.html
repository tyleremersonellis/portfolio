Initial condition of my grandfather's boat built in the 1950's.

Project Details / Background
A current project I am working on is restoring my grandfather’s old 1950s wooden boat. This boat came out of an old collapsing barn on my grandfather's farm. Overtime it had been chewed on by groundhogs, had holes kicked in it by a bull, and at one point looked to have been home to a family of mice. So far I've repaired and reinforced the hull, replaced the damaged planks and trim, and refinished the hull with fresh stain, paint, and several layers of UV protecting varnish. I've also added an electric steering system and trolling motor to the boat. In the future, I’m hoping to restore the original Evinrude two-stroke gas engine it was once paired with to bring back full original working condition.
